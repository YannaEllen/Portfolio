Web Design for Everybody: Basics of Web Development & Coding

This website is my final project on the Specialization I'm taking by the University of Michigan available on the Coursera website. Web Design for Everybody: Basics of Web Development & Coding was taught by the professor Colleen Van Lent. In this specialization I had to go through five courses: Introduction to HTML5, Introduction to CSS3, Interactivity with Java Script, Advanced Styling with Responsive Design and last but not least, Web Design for Everybody Capstone.

"It took me around 3 months to finish this course and it was a great experience. I learned a lot and it was something I wanted to do for a long time. I'm happy I finally managed to have time to do it."

My name is Yanna, I'm 26 years old and I'm from Brazil. I came to the USA to work as an Au Pair, and in order to stay here for two years we must take a course during the first year. Many Au Pairs take English courses during their stay here, however, since I was an English Teacher in Brazil I decided I wanted to do something completely different from what I was used to. I always wanted to know more about programming and technology. After talking to some friends from the area I decided to try Web Design.
